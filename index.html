My FAVOURITE Cinemas List

1.KGF : Trailer Collection $1,200 to $1,250 crore worldwide

The K.G.F. (Kolar Gold Fields) film series is a Kannada-language period action series created by Prashanth Neel, starring Yash as the protagonist, Raja Krishnappa Bairya, known as Rocky. The story follows Rocky's rise from poverty to becoming a powerful figure in the Kolar Gold Fields, and it was released in two installments, with a third planned. The first film, K.G.F: Chapter 1, was released in 2018 and set the stage for the story, while K.G.F: Chapter 2 continued Rocky's story in 2022.

2.Kantara: collection stands at 730.54 crores

Kantara is a 2022 Indian action thriller film written and directed by Rishab Shetty, known for its depiction of traditional culture and folklore from coastal Karnataka. It tells the story of a Kambala champion who confronts a forest officer, exploring themes of land, man, and nature through the lens of local beliefs and traditions like Bhootha Kola. The film was a major commercial success and was followed by a prequel, Kantara: Chapter 1, released in 2025.

3.Dude : 11 crore (US$1.3 million)

Dude is a 2025 Tamil romantic drama film directed by debutant Keerthiswaran and starring Pradeep Ranganathan and Mamitha Baiju.
